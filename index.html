Healthy Food Groups

Dark Leafy Greens

Examples

Kale, Swiss Chard, Collard Greens, Turnip Greens, Spinach.

Nutrients

Folate,Zinc,Calcium,Iron,Magnesium,Vitamin C, Fiber

Potential Health Impact

May reduce risk of chronic illnesses including heart disease and type 2 diabetes.

Berries
Examples

Blueberries, Blackberries, Cranberries, Strawberries, Raspberries.

Nutrients

Fiber,Antioxidants,Vitamins,Minerals

Potential Health Impact

May reduce risk of heart disease,cancer and other inflammatory conditions. May be effective in treating digestive and immune-related disorders.

Green Tea
Examples

Green Tea.

Nutrients

Antioxidants, Polyphenolic compounds with strong anti-inflammatory effects.

Potential Health Impact

May help protect against heart disease, diabetes and cancer.

Eggs
Examples

Eggs.

Nutrients

B Vitamins, Choline, Selenium, Vitamin A, Iron, Phosphorus, Antioxidants (Zeaxanthin an Lutein)

Potential Health Impact

Rich in high quality protein and unique Antioxidants.

LEGUMES
Examples

Beans (including Soy), Lentils, Peas, Peanuts and Alfalfa

Nutrients

B Vitamins, Various minerals, Protein and Fiber

Potential Health Impact

May improve type 2 diabetes management and reduce blood pressure and cholesterol.

NUTS and SEEDS
Examples

Almonds, Pecans, Pistachios, Walnuts, Cashews, Brazil Nuts, Macadamia Nuts, Peanuts, Sunflower Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Chia Seeds, Flaxseeds, Hemp Seeds

Nutrients

Rich in Fiber, Vegetarian Protein and Heart-healhy fats

Potential Health Impact

May provide protective effect against heart disease.
